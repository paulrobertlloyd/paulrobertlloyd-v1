Just as I started to write "I'd like to see each of these self contained in their own circles..." I saw your comment with the link to Radio Pop. I like those much better... something bugs me about the text that extends beyond the solid circles in A, 6, and both 5s.

I'm horribly nit picky. Probably explains why I was never good at logo design in my ad agency days. Grin.
